Contributor Spotlight

The photographers, illustrators, and models who trusted Uncalloused with their feet. Real, unretouched, unreserved. This page grows after each contest cycle. It starts with the one who was already here.

artist in residence
artist in residence · illustration
Amir

Amir draws feet the way anatomists used to draw hearts — with the conviction that the structure itself is the meaning, that you don't need metaphor when the form already carries everything.

His work appears throughout Uncalloused without announcement. You find it the way you find anything worth finding: by going a little deeper than you planned.

He doesn't title his drawings. He signs them, which is enough.

— sole to sole, pencil on paper
