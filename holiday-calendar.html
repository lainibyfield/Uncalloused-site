The Foot Holiday Calendar

Every verified foot observance, meme date, awareness month, and cultural moment worth knowing — organized by month. Bookmark this. Return when something comes up. The product calendar features these; this page is the reference layer behind it.

Terms marked with a ·g· have entries in the Glossary of Sole.

January
Jan 1
National First Foot Day
Scottish tradition — the first person to cross a threshold in the new year brings luck. The foot that enters first matters.
verified
Jan 23
National Measure Your Feet Day
Literal self-knowledge. Most adults haven't measured their feet since childhood. Sizing affects posture, gait, and joint health.
health

Feb 12
Valen-Toes Day
Ours. We claimed it. The pre-Valentine warm-up for people who send foot pics instead of roses. Love yourself from the tips up.

All month
POP Month — Prosthetics, Orthotics & Pedorthics
Annual awareness month for the professionals and communities working with adaptive devices. See also: orthotics, Limb & Difference.
prosthetics
Mar 15
National Shoe the World Day
Charity-focused. Donate a spare pair. The foot that gives a shoe carries as much meaning as the foot that receives one.
verified
Late March
Odor-Eaters National Rotten Sneaker Contest
50-year-old media event. Kids compete at Ripley's Times Square for stinkiest shoes. Date shifts annually — last week of March. See: bromodosis.
verified

All month
National Foot Health Awareness Month
Recognized by the APMA. Odor control, nail care, diabetic foot, and preventive screening are core themes. See: Foot Anatomy 101.

Limb Loss & Limb Difference Awareness Month (LLLDAM)
The month Uncalloused widens the tent furthest. 10% of judge-pass revenue donated to Amputee Coalition peer support. See: Limb & Difference.
prosthetics
Apr 17–23
National Feet Week (UK)
Annual UK awareness week. Podiatry clinics, NHS foot screenings, and public education campaigns.

Urban Dictionary sourced. Internet-only. Adult humor only — not on mainstream calendars. We note it; we don't feature it in the product calendar.
